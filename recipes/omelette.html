Omelette

Description:

A customizable meatlovers style omelette with egg, cheese, bacon, tomato + any meat you want to add

Steps:

  1. break 5 eggs into a cup
  2. whisk the eggs
  3. add a handful of grated cheese to the the cup
  4. mix it together
  5. heat the pan to a low heat
  6. prepare cut tomato and cut bacon strips into smaller pieces with a pair of scissors
  7. you can include more meat such as ham, chicken, etc if you want a meatlovers style omelette
  8. add the bacon to your cup
  9. mix and pour into the pan
  10. stir the ingredients initially to even the distribution
  11. avoid stirring to let the omelette form
  12. as soon as it's stable enough add your cut tomato and flip
  13. Take it out of the pan when the omelette is set but still moist
